## Reiner Gamma

1930 GMT 
Variety of colours witnessed on the surface and noted that as they look more at the moon it appears to be gradually getting more brown

1942 GMT
Observation, identification of mare imbrium looks different from another angle compared to the western limb view, the mountian chains that form its eastern side are very prominant but on the western side it looks like a large healing wound, big bump of a mountain chain. Striking features that is visible on earth. 

2045 GMT
Support swap, window viewers swapping with cabin support

2051 GMT
Crew swap happening. Docking window view is showing quite a different perspective but as the astraunaught moved to this window from the terminator and then oriental 400mm camera lens they noticed a significant difference.
